Course Meeting Times
--------------------

Lectures: 2 sessions / week, 1.5 hours / session

Recitations: 1 session / week, 1 hour / session

Prerequisites
-------------

The course is proof-based and assumes that students are comfortable with discrete mathematics, optimization, topology, and probability theory. Previous exposure to economic modeling is helpful. The prerequisite for the course is a substantial economics background or a strong math background with some economics coursework. Â
